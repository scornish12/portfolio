Profile

My name is Sam Cornish and I am an experienced, senior, UX designer with skills and backgrounds in graphic design and branding. I am formally educated in Graphic Design as I have achieved both a Bachelor and Masters degree from University Centre Farnborough and Arts University Bournemouth respectively. My experience has taught me discipline and I have developed an excellent eye for detail. I also pride myself on being available to and supportive of others that I work with. I believe it is hugely important to foster and develop working relationships especially in the design sector as so many roles involve working within a design team.
